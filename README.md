- **Competition**: RoboCup@Home 2026
- **League**: Open Platform League (OPL)
- **Location**: Incheon, Korea
- **Date**: June 30 - July 6, 2026

## General Rules
- All teams must comply with RoboCup@Home regulations
- Robots must operate autonomously unless otherwise specified
- Teams are responsible for their robot's safety and behavior
- Fair play and sportsmanship are mandatory

## Technical Specifications
- Robot platform: Open (any platform allowed)
- Communication: Standard ROS or approved alternative
- Sensors: Any combination allowed within safety limits
- Power: On-board battery or approved power source

## Test Procedures
Refer to the specific test documentation for:
- Task specifications
- Evaluation criteria
- Scoring methodology
- Time limits

## Safety Guidelines
- All robots must pass safety inspection before competition
- Emergency stop (E-stop) mechanism mandatory
- Maximum noise levels must be respected
- Teams liable for any damage caused by their robots

## Team Requirements
- Minimum team size: 2 members
- Maximum team size: 5 members
- Valid team registration required
- All team members must sign code of conduct

## Scoring System
- Points awarded based on task completion
- Bonus points for exceptional performance
- Safety violations may result in penalties
- Refer to specific test scoring rules
